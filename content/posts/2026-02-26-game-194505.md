Hungry Studio's flagship title, Block Blast!, has been recognized as the "Best Puzzle Game" at the Sensor Tower APAC Awards 2025, a testament to its global appeal and innovative gameplay. With over 70 million daily active users and available in more than 200 countries, Block Blast! has captivated players with its unique drag-match-disappear mechanics that blend accessibility with structured challenges.

The success of Block Blast! is not just about its gameplay; it's also about how Hungry Studio has localized its strategies to resonate with diverse audiences across the Asia-Pacific region. From prefecture-based high-score challenges in Japan to celebrity collaborations in Korea, these initiatives have transformed the game from a global sensation into a beloved local favorite.

As we look ahead, Hungry Studio's commitment to player feedback and data-driven innovation promises to push the boundaries of casual gaming even further. What new heights will Block Blast! reach in the coming years, and how will it continue to evolve in the ever-changing landscape of mobile gaming?
