PC gamers, rejoice! A delightful new puzzle game called Nested is now available for free download as a demo on Steam. This charming game, designed by Nathan Sioui, emerged as one of the winners of the GMTK 2024 Game Jam, showcasing a unique blend of whimsy and clever mechanics that will surely capture the hearts of indie game enthusiasts.

Nested invites players to engage in a colorful world filled with nesting dolls, where puzzles are solved by stacking these dolls in the correct order based on size and color. The games innovative mechanics, such as color-inverting buttons and interactive stack nodes, promise to deliver a cozy yet challenging experience. As you navigate through various levels, youll encounter creative obstacles that require both strategy and a keen eye for detail.

With the full game set to release in August 2026, now is the perfect time to dive into the demo and experience the magic of Nested for yourself. Will you be able to master the art of stacking and unlock the secrets of this enchanting puzzle adventure?
