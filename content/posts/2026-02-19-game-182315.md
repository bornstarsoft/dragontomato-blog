The upcoming indie game Hakoniwa Electric promises to be a delightful escape for puzzle enthusiasts and casual gamers alike. Developed by Anettai, this cozy isometric puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of Akari, a novice shopkeeper tasked with managing her grandfather's electronics shop while he enjoys a tropical vacation. With its charming visuals and relaxing gameplay, Hakoniwa Electric is set to light up the gaming scene when it launches on Steam in 2026.

In Hakoniwa Electric, players will explore 12 beautifully crafted isometric stages, each brimming with intricate wiring puzzles that are as adorable as they are engaging. The game emphasizes a stress-free experience, allowing players to solve puzzles at their own pace without the pressure of time limits or complex calculations. As you progress, youll get glimpses into Akari's life through her Monthly Diary, adding a personal touch to the gameplay.

With its focus on relaxation and creativity, Hakoniwa Electric is more than just a game; it's an invitation to unwind and enjoy the simple pleasures of life. Will you join Akari and Lamp on this heartwarming journey? Stay tuned for more updates as we approach the release date!
