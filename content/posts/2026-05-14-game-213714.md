The gaming community is buzzing with the recent launch of Phase 2 for "Neverness to Everness," which went live on May 13, 2026. Despite the ongoing AI controversy surrounding the game's assets, Hotta Studio is pushing forward with exciting new content. This update introduces a limited S-class character, Hotori, and a fresh event lineup that runs until June 3, promising players a wealth of new experiences.

Hotori is featured on the Misty Tipsy Style Limited Board, where players can engage in the game's unique gacha mechanics. The player-friendly pity system ensures that if you dont pull Hotori within 70 rolls, the odds improve, making it a more rewarding experience. Additionally, the Bright Moon Special Arc Research Program offers boosted rates for the S-rank arc, Marching Beyond Time, enhancing the gameplay further.

Looking ahead, players can anticipate the introduction of new characters like Lacrimosa and Chaos, both of whom have been teased through drip marketing. As the game evolves, it will be interesting to see how Hotta Studio addresses the AI controversy and what impact it will have on player engagement and trust. Will the new content be enough to win back skeptics, or will the shadow of AI-generated assets linger?
