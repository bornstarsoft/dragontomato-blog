In a significant move for the gaming industry, Shift Up, the developer behind Stellar Blade, has acquired Unbound, the new studio founded by the legendary Shinji Mikami, known for creating the Resident Evil franchise. This acquisition marks a pivotal moment as both companies share a vision for innovative game development, emphasizing strong creative direction and high-quality execution.

Founded in 2023, Unbound has been quietly working on an original IP with a team of around 50 employees. The partnership aims to leverage the strengths of both studios, with Shift Up providing development resources and publishing support for Unbound's future titles. Mikami expressed excitement about the collaboration, highlighting the synergy between him and Shift Up's CEO, Hyung Tae Kim.
