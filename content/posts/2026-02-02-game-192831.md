If you're a PlayStation Plus subscriber, there's a hidden gem waiting for you: The Exit 8, a short yet spine-chilling puzzle game that has been making waves in the gaming community. Released as part of the January lineup, this indie title offers a unique blend of horror and puzzle-solving that is sure to captivate players looking for something different.

In The Exit 8, players navigate a hauntingly atmospheric Japanese metro tunnel, identifying various anomalies that could range from harmless discrepancies to unsettling images. The game's first-person perspective immerses you in a looping experience where each mistake could lead to a chilling consequence. With its easy trophies and a playtime of just a couple of hours, its a perfect fit for those looking for a quick yet memorable gaming session.

As players have noted, the mix of obvious and subtle anomalies keeps the gameplay engaging, even if some wish it lasted longer. For anyone with a PlayStation Plus Extra or Premium subscription, The Exit 8 is a must-try. Will you dare to uncover its mysteries?
