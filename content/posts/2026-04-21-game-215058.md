Japanese entertainment giant Toei Company is making waves in the gaming industry by launching its own gaming division, Toei Games. This strategic move marks a significant expansion for the company, which has been a staple in film, television, and animation since its founding in 1949. Known for iconic franchises like Kamen Rider and Super Sentai, Toei is now set to leverage its extensive experience in video production to create original gaming titles.

Toei Games aims to develop completely new intellectual properties (IP) rather than relying on existing franchises. The initial games will be released on PC via Steam, with plans to expand to home consoles later. This approach not only showcases Toei's commitment to innovation but also highlights the potential for unique gaming experiences that blend storytelling and technology, a hallmark of the companys legacy.

As CEO Fumio Yoshimura stated, the goal is to deliver Toei's unique entertainment experience to players around the world. With the gaming market continuing to grow, it will be fascinating to see how Toei's new venture unfolds and what fresh narratives they will introduce to the gaming community. Could this be the start of a new era for Toei and its fans?
