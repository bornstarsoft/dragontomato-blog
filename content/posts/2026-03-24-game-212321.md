Cursed Words, the upcoming roguelike puzzle game from Forlife Interactive and developer Buried Things, is set to release on April 2, 2026, on Steam PC. This game draws inspiration from the popular poker-like deckbuilder, Belatro, and promises to deliver a unique twist on word games by combining elements of Boggle and Scrabble with zany, rules-breaking mechanics. Players will use letter tiles to create words, but with a quirky twist that includes numbers, chess pieces, and other symbols.

The game features 11 playable characters, each with distinct powers and synergies, alongside randomized shops and upgrades. With over 300 stickers and stamps, players can expect a vast array of combinations for unique gameplay experiences. The developers have even released a free demo on Steam, allowing players to dive into this chaotic wordplay before the official launch.

As we look forward to the release of Cursed Words, it raises an intriguing question: how will this innovative approach to word games resonate with both casual gamers and hardcore puzzle enthusiasts? Will it carve out a niche in the crowded gaming landscape? Only time will tell.
