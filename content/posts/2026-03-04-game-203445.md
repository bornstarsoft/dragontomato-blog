Accidental Studios has unveiled the launch date for their highly anticipated psychological horror puzzle game, Subliminal, set to release on March 31st, 2026. This game promises to immerse players in a hauntingly beautiful world inspired by the unsettling mythology of liminal spaces and The Backrooms, where players must navigate through distorted memories and fears.

In Subliminal, players will manipulate light to reshape their environment and solve perspective-based puzzles that challenge their understanding of reality. Built using Unreal Engine 5, the game boasts hyper-realistic visuals and a suffocating atmosphere that keeps players on edge. The recently released demo on Steam has already captivated thousands, offering a glimpse into the chilling experience that awaits.

As we count down to the release, one can't help but wonder: how will Subliminal redefine the psychological horror genre and push the boundaries of interactive storytelling? Stay tuned for more updates as we delve deeper into this intriguing game.
