In a thrilling announcement for gaming enthusiasts, ININ Games and TAITO have revealed that the beloved 2D platformers, Spica Adventure and Parasol Stars: The Story of Bubble Bobble III, will be bundled together in a vibrant package titled Parasol Superstars. This exciting release marks a significant milestone as it will feature the first-ever worldwide console launch of Spica Adventure, set to hit digital platforms on April 14, 2026, for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4|5, and Xbox.

The combination of these two classic titles not only brings nostalgia to long-time fans but also introduces new players to the charming worlds of Spica Adventure and Parasol Stars. With their colorful graphics and engaging gameplay, this bundle promises to be a delightful experience for both casual gamers and platformer aficionados alike. As we await the release, it's clear that this is a momentous occasion for the gaming community.
