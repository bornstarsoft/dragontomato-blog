The gaming world is buzzing with excitement as Triangle Wave's innovative puzzle game, Stretchmancer, has been picked up by Offbrand Games. This title, which started as a Ludum Dare game jam winner, has evolved into a fully-fledged adventure that challenges players to think creatively while navigating through intricate puzzles using unique stretching mechanics. With its engaging premise and gameplay, Stretchmancer is poised to capture the hearts of puzzle enthusiasts everywhere.

Players will step into the shoes of a reptile-like character known as the Stretchmancer, who possesses the ability to stretch and compress surrounding matter. This mechanic not only allows for creative puzzle-solving but also enhances platforming elements, making for a dynamic gaming experience. As players embark on a quest to escape the clutches of the Claustro Empire, they will encounter voice-acted dialogue and an original soundtrack, adding depth to the narrative.

As Offbrand Games continues to build its portfolio, Stretchmancer marks a significant addition alongside titles like Rivals 2 and Aethermancer. With its unique gameplay and engaging story, it raises the question: could Stretchmancer redefine the standards for puzzle games in the industry? Stay tuned as we await its release on PC!
