Indie developer Business Goose Studios is set to release their highly anticipated puzzle game, Swan Song, on June 4th for Windows PC via Steam. This cozy yet emotional game invites players to engage with a unique blend of music and storytelling, making it a standout in the indie gaming scene.

In Swan Song, players will use musical notes to program a music box, solving puzzles that guide a swan through various levels. Each puzzle not only challenges your problem-solving skills but also unfolds a touching narrative about family, loss, and grief. As you progress, youll uncover the heartfelt story of the music boxs creator and the legacy he left behind, making every solved puzzle a step deeper into a moving tale.

With its combination of engaging gameplay and emotional depth, Swan Song promises to resonate with players looking for a meaningful gaming experience. Will you be ready to help the swan navigate through its poignant journey?
