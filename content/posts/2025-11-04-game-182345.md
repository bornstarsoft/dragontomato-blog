The excitement surrounding the Nintendo Switch 2 continues to build as two of its standout titles, Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza, have been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition not only highlights the quality of these games but also marks a significant moment for Nintendo as it showcases the potential of its new hardware in a competitive gaming landscape.

Both games have received multiple nominations, with Mario Kart World competing for 'Best Multiplayer' and Donkey Kong Bananza nominated for 'Best Audio Design'. As they face off against heavyweights like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei, its clear that Nintendo is making a strong statement with its latest offerings. The public voting is now open, and fans are eager to see how these titles will fare against their competition.

As we approach the announcement of the winners on November 20, it will be interesting to see if the Switch 2 can claim the crown in multiple categories. Will Nintendos latest innovations and beloved franchises secure their place in gaming history?
