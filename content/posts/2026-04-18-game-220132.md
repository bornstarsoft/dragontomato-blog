The 2026 BAFTA Games Awards showcased an impressive array of talent, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 taking home the coveted Best Game award. This year's ceremony highlighted the diversity and creativity in the gaming industry, as multiple titles received recognition across various categories.

Clair Obscur's success was complemented by AdHoc's Dispatch, which also won three awards, including Animation and Audio Achievement. The event celebrated not only big studios but also indie games, with Blue Prince winning the Game Design award, proving that innovation thrives in all corners of the gaming landscape. As we look ahead, it's exciting to consider what new narratives and experiences will emerge from this vibrant industry.
