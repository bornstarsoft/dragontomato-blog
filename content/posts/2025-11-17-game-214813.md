In an exciting announcement that has caught the attention of gamers worldwide, Game Pop Studio has revealed their upcoming title, "They Will Come." Set to launch in 2026 on PC via Steam, this steampunk horror adventure puzzle game promises to deliver a unique blend of immersive visuals and a gripping narrative. Players will step into the shoes of Little Benjamin, armed with a slingshot and electrobanger, as he navigates a world filled with towering automatons and the ominous Wretched Ones.

The games setting is a richly crafted alternate history, where players must solve intricate puzzles while evading danger in a hauntingly beautiful environment. The bittersweet story of family and survival adds emotional depth to the gameplay, ensuring that players are not just solving puzzles but are also invested in the fate of Little Benjamins family. With its unique art style and engaging mechanics, "They Will Come" is poised to stand out in the horror genre.

As we await its release, one cant help but wonder how this game will redefine the steampunk aesthetic in gaming. Will it set a new standard for horror adventures? Only time will tell!
