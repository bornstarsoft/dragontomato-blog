Exciting news for gamers! The beloved multiplayer 3D puzzle game, Fruit Mountain Party, is set to launch on Switch 2 and Switch in spring 2026. Currently available in Early Access on PC, this vibrant game invites players to engage in fruit battles and stack fruits to create bigger combinations, promising hours of fun and competition.

The game features an array of modes, including a heated online battle mode for up to four players, where you can challenge friends or players worldwide. Whether you prefer a serious time attack or a chaotic battle filled with surprises, Fruit Mountain Party has something for everyone. Plus, the single-player mode allows you to compete for high scores against players globally, making it a versatile choice for all types of gamers.
