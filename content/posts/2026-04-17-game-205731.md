In a world where technology and emotions intertwine, "Save My Scrap" offers players a unique experience that goes beyond mere gameplay. Set to launch on April 22, 2026, this visual novel and puzzle game invites you to repair a broken android, Harima, while navigating the complex relationships between him and his creator, Dr. Miomaru. The game promises to deliver not just mechanical challenges, but also a deeply emotional narrative that resonates with players.

Originally a free game titled "Fix My Junk," this upgraded version brings new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode, enhancing the storytelling experience. With its stunning cyberpunk art and heart-wrenching themes of connection and memory, "Save My Scrap" is poised to capture the hearts of gamers around the globe. As we await its release on multiple platforms, including Steam and consoles, one can't help but wonder how this game will redefine our understanding of relationships in a digital age.

Are you ready to dive into this emotional journey and explore the depths of human-android connections? Let's see how "Save My Scrap" unfolds!
