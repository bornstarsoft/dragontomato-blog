Hazard Pay, an intriguing pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, is set to launch this June for PlayStation 5, Switch, and PC. Developed by Smitner Studio and published by Numskull Games, this game promises to deliver a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative choice, all wrapped in a captivating retro aesthetic. Players will step into the role of a government cleanup specialist tasked with navigating a sinister research facility, where the stakes are high and the horrors lurking within are all too real.

Set against the backdrop of a Cold War-era state, Hazard Pay challenges players to not only solve intricate puzzles but also to make critical decisions that will affect the game's outcome. With an emphasis on environmental storytelling and branching narratives, players will uncover the dark secrets behind the experiments conducted at the Institut fr Proteintherapie. Will you choose to expose the truth, or will you become just another disposable tool of the system?

As we await the game's release, the demo available on PC offers a tantalizing glimpse into the gameplay mechanics and narrative depth that Hazard Pay has to offer. This game is not just about solving puzzles; it's about survival, choice, and the chilling atmosphere that comes with uncovering a hidden horror. What choices will you make when faced with the terrifying consequences of your actions?
