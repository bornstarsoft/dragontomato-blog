In an exciting development for retro gaming fans, Hyperkin has partnered with ModRetro to introduce premium color-matched controllers for the upcoming M64 console. The new Captain Premium Controllers are designed not only for durability but also for comfort, making them ideal for intense gaming sessions. With a robust 3-meter cable and an extension port for accessories like memory cards and rumble feedback, these controllers promise to elevate the gaming experience to new heights.

Slade Dude Suzuki, Lead Product Developer at Hyperkin, expressed the team's enthusiasm for this collaboration, emphasizing how the updated Captain Controller aligns perfectly with the innovative M64 console. With four new vibrant colorsGreen, Clear, Purple, and Redjoining the existing lineup, gamers can choose a controller that matches their style while enjoying the nostalgia of 64-bit gaming.

As we look forward to the release of these controllers later this year, one can't help but wonder: will this collaboration set a new standard for retro gaming accessories?
