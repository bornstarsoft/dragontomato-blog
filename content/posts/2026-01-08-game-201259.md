The newly launched puzzle game AiliA invites players into a captivating world where reality and reflection intertwine. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, this mirror-based visual puzzle game is now available on Steam for $11.99, with a 20% discount for early adopters. AiliA offers a unique gameplay experience that challenges players to manipulate mirrors not just as reflections, but as interactive portals that alter their surroundings and reveal new paths.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend the real and mirrored worlds. The game features over 100 puzzles, including intricate challenges that utilize water reflections and recursive mirror structures. Additionally, players can engage in thrilling boss encounters that cleverly incorporate mirror mechanics, making each level a fresh and engaging experience.

For those eager to dive into this enchanting puzzle adventure, a free demo is available on Steam. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or a newcomer to the genre, AiliA promises to deliver a memorable journey filled with mystery and discovery. What hidden stories will you uncover in the reflections of Aria and Lia's memories?
